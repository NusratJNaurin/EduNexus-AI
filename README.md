# EduNexus AI
> A Collaborative Academic Knowledge Workspace & Research Sandbox for Qatar University — turning dense research papers into navigable, conversational, and measurable knowledge.

## Short Description

EduNexus AI is an intelligent, collaborative academic workspace and research sandbox designed for Qatar University to turn dense research papers into navigable, conversational, and measurable knowledge. Rather than scrolling through static PDFs, the platform gives users three connected features: interactive visual mapping of research methodologies and concepts, precision extraction of key statistical metrics like p-values, and a completely source-grounded AI companion that prevents hallucinated claims. Designed to improve research literacy, EduNexus AI helps learners develop the skills of a researcher by identifying methodologies, evaluating evidence, and developing a deeper understanding of academic literature. Through its Socratic-style "Viva Pod" oral defense simulator, students are challenged to explain, justify, and defend their interpretations of research findings. It provides a safe, structured environment for students to practice the habits of rigorous academic thinking before they are applied in real-world settings.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router) with React 19
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4 with semantic design tokens (maroon & gold palette)
- **UI Components:** shadcn/ui
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Fonts:** Geist Sans & Geist Mono (next/font)
- **Database (schema-ready):** Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security
- **Deployment:** Vercel
